How to Play

The Goal:

It's simple: guess the same word at the same time!

How It Works:

  1. Both players secretly enter a word.
  2. The words are revealed. If they match? you win!
  3. If not, don't worry! Use those words to try and think of a new word.
  4. Keep going, until...  MindMeld!

Example:

If one player said "sand" and the other said "water", you might both try "beach" to find the middle ground!
